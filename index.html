About This Project

Abstract Marine describes photography by Ellery Littleton, taken of reflections (mostly of fishing boats), around Vancouver Island waterfronts (Port Alberni in particular). The photos reflect the magical interplay of light and water; creating images that are both obvious and enigmatic. Some of these images create the illusion of exotic landscapes, with water, beaches, shorelines and sky. Many of them feature horizontal lines which can highlight the impression of coastal vistas. Ellery is constantly captivated by beautiful, mysterious reflections which are illusory, changeable and fascinating, allowing the imagination to see what it will.
